What is Undertale?

Undertale is a unique RPG where you can choose to befriend or battle enemies.

Learn More

An AU (Alternate Universe) is a concept where an alternate version of a universe like Undertale is created. It's a creative world where characters are redesigned with new traits or stories.

Popular Undertale AUs include SwapTale, Swapfell, StorySwap, AfterTale, XTale, UnderSwap, FellTale, HorrorTale, ErrorTale, InkTale, MysteryTale, Outertale, Glitchtale, DoomTale, CrossTale, Underfell, DustTale, Underfresh, Reapertale, Epictale, NightmareTale. These AUs explore Undertale's characters and world in different ways, each with its unique characteristics.

Alternate universes (AUs) in Undertale reimagine the world with different twists on the characters, storylines, and settings. These AUs explore "what if" scenarios, where familiar characters take on new personalities, roles, or alignments, and the events of the game unfold in unique ways. Each AU creates its own set of rules and lore, providing fans with a fresh take on the Undertale universe. These universes offer endless creative possibilities and expand the original narrative in exciting ways.

In Undertale AUs, the roles of monsters often vary depending on the universe's theme and character traits. Monsters may take on different personalities, powers, and functions in each AU. Some AUs depict them as darker, more ruthless, while others might see them with reversed roles or personalities. Their roles often define the story's core and shape the relationships between characters, their purposes, and the world they inhabit.

In Undertale, Pacifist and Genocide routes drastically alter the narrative and character interactions. The Pacifist route is filled with kindness, sparing characters and leading to a positive outcome. The Genocide route, however, involves eliminating all monsters, resulting in a darker world where characters become hostile. These paths influence the ending and the tone of the story, showing how the player's choices shape the game's world.
